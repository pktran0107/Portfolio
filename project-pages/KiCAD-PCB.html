KiCAD | PCB Design
Project Details / Background
As part of an ongoing personal project, I'm learning to design and simulate a custom PCB to strengthen my understanding of schematic capture, layout design, and circuit simulation. The board is intended for use in a simple electronics or in robotics applications. Learning KiCAD will serve as hand-on experience and practice in working with component libraries, routing strategies, and learning about design rules for manufacturing.
I am also completing a KiCAD PCB Design course on Udemy to build a strong foundation in professional board design workflows. The course covers schematic capture, multi-layer PCB layout and routing, complementing my project and preparing my for real-world PCB manufacturing processes.
