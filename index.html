My Background / About Me
Hi, I'm Peter - I have a B.S. in Computer Engineering from Cal State Fullerton with a passion for discovering and building embedded systems that bridge hardware and software. I enjoy working with microcontrollers, designing PCBs, and exploring how hardware and software come together to solve real world problems.
During my University years, I led and contributed to projects like the UPS: Ultrasonic Parametric speaker system, a miniature traffic light controller, and helped in the development of the robotic arm for TitanRover. I also worked as a research assistant for Dr. Rakesh Mahto - Professor of Electrical & Computer Engineering - where I researched solar-powered microautonomous drones built for forest fire detection using machine learning.
These days, I'm continuing to build up my skills in embedded systems, PCB design, and hardware engineering. I am always looking for new ways to learn, build, and create technology that makes a difference.
